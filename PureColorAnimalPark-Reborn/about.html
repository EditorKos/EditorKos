WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE?

This is the fanmade revival project of PureColorAnimalPark’s puzzle games, which has been long lost.

 This website is a memorial of these wonderful puzzle games, all created by 若月あおい .
It exists because by the time of writing this article, the original host site - PureColorAnimalPark is no longer online.

Most of the games have been archived and presented here, you can play these classic games again!

These games have cute graphic designs and interesting gameplay. They deserve to live on and to be enjoyed by everyone.
Please show your appreciation to the original creator 若月あおい by visiting her blogs!
